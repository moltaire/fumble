About Vattenfall:
Vattenfall is one of Europe's leading producers and retailers of electricity and heat. With 21,000 employees across Sweden, Germany, the Netherlands, Denmark, and the UK, we have been electrifying industries and powering societies for over a century. Today, our mission is clear: working for fossil-freedom.

Within the Business Unit Hydro Power Plants Germany, we operate the country's largest storage portfolio and play a critical role in balancing short-term power markets. Our pump storage plants provide flexibility and reliability — key enablers of the energy transition.

The Data Innovation team develops and operates a Databricks-based analytics and machine learning platform to deliver actionable insights and support strategic, commercial, and operational decisions. We collaborate closely across the business to improve efficiency, reduce emissions, and accelerate the transition to a sustainable energy system.
